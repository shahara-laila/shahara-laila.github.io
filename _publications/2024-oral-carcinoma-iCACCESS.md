abstract: >
  This paper presents a novel approach to detecting oral carcinoma using YOLO-based object detection
  with box annotation. We develop a detection pipeline that identifies and localizes cancerous lesions
  in clinical oral photographs, demonstrating that YOLO architectures can achieve accurate bounding-box
  level localization of oral carcinoma with limited annotated training data.
